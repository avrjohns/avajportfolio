About Me

Filmmaker.
Engineer.
Creator.

I'm a Computer Science: Game Design student at UC Santa Cruz with 8+ years of experience in video production and a strong foundation in software engineering — from Python and C to C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

From producing 1M+ view content for UCSC Admissions to hand-coding websites and building games, I work at the intersection of technology and storytelling.

As Outreach Officer for the Game Developers Association (GDA) at UCSC, I connect students with game industry professionals, coordinate events and partnerships, and serve as the bridge between the club and outside organizations.

As a crew member of the Film Production Coalition (FPC) at UCSC, I work on-set across short film productions as camera operator, boom operator, and video editor — from shoot through post.
